My name is Dawn Ferguson! I'm an HCDE masters student, visual artist, & educator. As a 3D visual artist I am excited to learn Rhino and play with processes.

Assignment 1:Construction Kit

Working off the idea of a geodome I started to design my consturction kit to highlight the shapes made by negative space

First test cut fit nicely thanks to the measurements taken with the digital calipers. By usig mm grid and ruler in Illustrator I was able to design the parts with the appropriate size from the beginning.

Steps Taken: 1.Identified shape to explore 2.Cut out paper pieces to understand angles 3.Started designing shapes in Illustrator by combining shapes and using the scissor tool to eliminate unwanted lines 4.Test cut on the laser cutter. I was very grateful for students who were nearby so that I could observe the process before trying it on my own. 5.First test fit!! I played around with the lengths of parts and experimented with the size, but ultimately returned to a larger unit because of the cardboard limitations.
